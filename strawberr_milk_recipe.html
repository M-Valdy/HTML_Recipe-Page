Strawberry Milk

Yifang's Strawberry Milk is a creamy blend of fresh strawberries and milk, perfect for a refreshing treat. You can find it on their menu along with other fruit teas and milk options..

Ingredients

For medium

  • 220g of Ice
  • 60mL of Sugar Syrup (100%)
  • 50g of Strawberry Chunks
  • 30g of Strawberry Jam
  • 130mL of Milk
  • 40mL of Cold Water
  • Approximately 2g

For large

  • 270g Ice
  • 75mL Sugar Syrup (100%)
  • 60g of Strawberry Chunks
  • 40g of Strawberry Jam
  • 180mL of Milk
  • 50mL of Cold Water
  • Approximately 3g

Instructions

  1. Scoop some ice using a medium sized ice scoop, then put it in the blender
  2. Put the sugar and the ice into the blender. Make sure to check the chart for proper proportions
  3. Put the strawberry chunks in the blender
  4. Put the strawberry jam in the blender
  5. Measure the amount of milk with a cup and put the milk in the blender
  6. Add some cold water into the blender and blend
  7. Combine the whipped cream along with the ingredients that customers ask for into the cup
  8. Wipe the edges, seal the cup, shake the drink and serve
